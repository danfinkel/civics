1. Premises

1.1  Landlord hereby leases to Tenant, and Tenant hereby leases from Landlord, the residential premises located at 247 Elm Street, Apartment 3B, Boston, Massachusetts 02119 (the "Premises"), for use as a private residence only.

1.2  The Premises consist of a two-bedroom apartment on the third floor of the building located at 247 Elm Street, Boston, MA 02119. Tenant acknowledges having inspected the Premises and agrees to accept them in their current condition, subject to any agreed-upon repairs noted in Exhibit A (not included in this synthetic document).

2. Lease Term

2.1  This lease shall commence on February 1, 2026 and shall terminate on January 31, 2027, unless sooner terminated in accordance with the provisions hereof (the "Term"). Tenant shall vacate the Premises no later than 11:59 PM on the last day of the Term unless the parties have executed a written renewal or extension.

3. Rent

3.1  Tenant agrees to pay Landlord the sum of $1,650.00 per month as rent for the Premises. Rent shall be due and payable on the first (1st) day of each calendar month during the Term. Rent shall be paid by check, money order, or electronic transfer to the address of Landlord listed above or to such other address as Landlord may designate in writing.

3.2  If rent is not received within five (5) days of the due date, Tenant shall pay a late fee of $50.00. Acceptance of late rent or late fees shall not constitute a waiver of Landlord's right to enforce timely payment in the future.

4. Security Deposit

4.1  Upon execution of this Agreement, Tenant shall deposit with Landlord the sum of $1,650.00 as a security deposit. The security deposit shall be held in accordance with Massachusetts General Laws Chapter 186, Section 15B. Landlord shall return the security deposit, less any lawful deductions, within thirty (30) days of the termination of this lease.

5. Occupants

5.1  The Premises shall be occupied solely by the Tenant named above and the following additional occupants: Sofia Ramirez-Chen (minor child). No other persons shall occupy the Premises without the prior written consent of Landlord.

6. Utilities

6.1  Tenant shall be responsible for payment of all utilities including electricity, gas, and internet service. Water and trash removal are included in the monthly rent. Tenant shall establish utility accounts in Tenant's own name prior to the commencement date.

7. Execution

The parties have executed this Residential Lease Agreement as of the date first written below. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to the Premises and supersedes all prior understandings, negotiations, and agreements.
